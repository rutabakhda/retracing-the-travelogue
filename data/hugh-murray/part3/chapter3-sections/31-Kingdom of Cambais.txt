This is a great kingdom to the west ; the people idolaters, with a language of their own, and a ruler subject to no other. Here the north-star appears more fully, as it always does the farther you go westward. This region is rich and productive, particularly in excellent indigo; also cotton, both raw and manufactured, which is exported to many other countries. I may add, many hides are dressed, and worked with a skill nowhere surpassed ; also other articles too tedious to enumerate. The foreign merchants bring mostly gold, silver, and brass. The people are not pirates, but honest ; living by trade and manufacture.And now I will tell you of Semenat.
